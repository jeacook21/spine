#bookclub

Enjoyable family saga. Elizabeth Zott the protagonist did not feel real. She is too perfect. She represents the ideal modern female. Independent and courageous.

Book gives a good view of mid century sexism and the challenges women face.

I found the portrait of Zott as too perfect almost decision itself. She never shouted at Mad, never bullied, never sort revenge. She sets a standard that’s impossible to reach.
